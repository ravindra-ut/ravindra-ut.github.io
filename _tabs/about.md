## Hey, I'm Ravindra.

I build language models that understand infrastructure. Currently Head of NLP at Flip AI, where I built a 34B-parameter LLM trained on observability data — logs, metrics, traces, events — that powers the core intelligence of the product. I was first author on our technical report, *System of Intelligent Actors*.

Before Flip, I was at Meta working on multimodal models for WhatsApp commerce, and at AWS shipping NLP services on Amazon Comprehend — PII detection, multilingual sentiment analysis, financial event extraction. That stretch produced a few patents and a lot of lessons about what it takes to make ML work at scale.

## What I write about

This blog is where I think out loud about the things I'm working through: reinforcement learning, inference optimization, evaluation design, multi-modal agents, and the general challenge of getting models to do useful things reliably.

I'm less interested in hype and more interested in what actually works in production.

## The backstory

My career started in a very different place — writing C and Erlang at Cisco, building protocol parsers and voice systems for ISR routers. From there I was the first employee at 1click.io, where I built real-time WebRTC video infrastructure from scratch. The company was later acquired by Freshworks.

The thread connecting all of it: building systems that hold up when things get messy. Networks were messy then. Production ML is messy now.
